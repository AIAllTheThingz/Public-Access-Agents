# Completion Evidence

## Purpose

Record profile decisions, selected standards, commands, results, limitations, review, and production-readiness status.

## Requirements

### AIAGENT-EVID-001

**Requirement:** Record selected profiles and packages.

**Expected evidence:** completion result.

### AIAGENT-EVID-002

**Requirement:** Record risk, scope, decisions, and changed files.

**Expected evidence:** validation output.

### AIAGENT-EVID-003

**Requirement:** Record exact validation commands and outcomes.

**Expected evidence:** risk record.

### AIAGENT-EVID-004

**Requirement:** Distinguish implemented, validated, reviewed, approved, and operationally verified states.

**Expected evidence:** review record.

### AIAGENT-EVID-005

**Requirement:** List checks not run, limitations, residual risk, and follow-up actions.

**Expected evidence:** limitations and follow-up.

### AIAGENT-EVID-006

**Requirement:** Link reviewer, approval, exception, and production-readiness records where required.

**Expected evidence:** limitations and follow-up.

## Decision gate

Do not close this area while required evidence is missing or material uncertainty is concealed.

## Exceptions

Use the governance exception process. An exception does not erase the underlying risk or the need for an accountable owner.

## Completion boundary

The presence of this standard is not evidence that the requirement was implemented or validated.
