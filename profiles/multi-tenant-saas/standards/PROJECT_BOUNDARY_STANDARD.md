# Project Boundary Standard

## Purpose

Define users, interfaces, data, trust, deployment, ownership, and non-goals before implementation.

## Requirements

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-001

**Requirement:** Define why the Multi-Tenant SaaS profile applies and what is out of scope.

**Expected evidence:** profile rationale.

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-002

**Requirement:** Identify users, operators, callers, targets, and support owners.

**Expected evidence:** system-context or architecture record.

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-003

**Requirement:** Document trust boundaries, interfaces, data, identities, and deployment environments.

**Expected evidence:** scope and non-goals.

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-004

**Requirement:** Identify primary and secondary profiles and their directory scopes.

**Expected evidence:** owner and interface list.

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-005

**Requirement:** Record assumptions, non-goals, and unresolved ownership.

**Expected evidence:** reassessment trigger.

### SAAS-BOUNDARY-006

**Requirement:** Reassess boundaries when architecture, deployment, data, or users change.

**Expected evidence:** reassessment trigger.

## Decision gate

Do not close this area while required evidence is missing or material uncertainty is concealed.

## Exceptions

Use the governance exception process. An exception does not erase the underlying risk or the need for an accountable owner.

## Completion boundary

The presence of this standard is not evidence that the requirement was implemented or validated.
