# Architecture and Decision Standard

## Purpose

Record components, dependencies, state, failure modes, compatibility, and material trade-offs.

## Requirements

### AUTO-ARCH-001

**Requirement:** Define components, responsibilities, dependencies, state, and failure modes.

**Expected evidence:** architecture diagram or narrative.

### AUTO-ARCH-002

**Requirement:** Record material decisions and alternatives.

**Expected evidence:** decision records.

### AUTO-ARCH-003

**Requirement:** Define compatibility, migration, and rollback constraints.

**Expected evidence:** dependency and state map.

### AUTO-ARCH-004

**Requirement:** Separate public contracts from implementation details.

**Expected evidence:** failure and recovery analysis.

### AUTO-ARCH-005

**Requirement:** Define lifecycle, cancellation, cleanup, and recovery behavior.

**Expected evidence:** compatibility notes.

### AUTO-ARCH-006

**Requirement:** Update architecture records with behavior-changing work.

**Expected evidence:** compatibility notes.

## Decision gate

Do not close this area while required evidence is missing or material uncertainty is concealed.

## Exceptions

Use the governance exception process. An exception does not erase the underlying risk or the need for an accountable owner.

## Completion boundary

The presence of this standard is not evidence that the requirement was implemented or validated.
