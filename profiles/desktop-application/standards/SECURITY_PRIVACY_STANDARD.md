# Security and Privacy Standard

## Purpose

Identify trust boundaries, privileges, sensitive data, abuse, authorization, secrets, and privacy obligations.

## Requirements

### DESKTOP-SEC-001

**Requirement:** Identify sensitive data, identities, privileges, and trust boundaries.

**Expected evidence:** threat or abuse analysis.

### DESKTOP-SEC-002

**Requirement:** Enforce authorization at the protected operation or object.

**Expected evidence:** authorization tests.

### DESKTOP-SEC-003

**Requirement:** Validate untrusted input and bound resource use.

**Expected evidence:** input and boundary tests.

### DESKTOP-SEC-004

**Requirement:** Keep secrets and sensitive data out of source, logs, errors, and examples.

**Expected evidence:** secret and log review.

### DESKTOP-SEC-005

**Requirement:** Define privacy, retention, deletion, and support-access behavior where applicable.

**Expected evidence:** privacy review.

### DESKTOP-SEC-006

**Requirement:** Test denied, invalid, boundary, abuse, and failure behavior.

**Expected evidence:** residual risk.

## Decision gate

Do not close this area while required evidence is missing or material uncertainty is concealed.

## Exceptions

Use the governance exception process. An exception does not erase the underlying risk or the need for an accountable owner.

## Completion boundary

The presence of this standard is not evidence that the requirement was implemented or validated.
