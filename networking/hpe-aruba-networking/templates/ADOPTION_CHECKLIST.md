# HPE Aruba Networking Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Stable device, site, role, owner, hardware, serial/stable ID, and environment recorded
- [ ] AOS-CX, AOS-Switch/ProVision, or Comware family and exact release verified
- [ ] Central/controller, group/template, source of truth, local overrides, and conflict policy mapped
- [ ] VSX, VSF, stack, gateway, uplink, and other redundancy relationships inventoried
- [ ] Interfaces, optics, VLAN/VRF, STP, LAG, routing, overlay, ACL, QoS, and dependencies mapped
- [ ] AAA, certificates, management VRF, console/OOB, logging, telemetry, and break glass defined
- [ ] Hardware/software/controller/license lifecycle, support, security, and source-review date recorded
- [ ] Backup/checkpoint, boot fallback, configuration restore, spare, rollback, and escalation tested or bounded
- [ ] Syntax/schema, topology, controller conflict, peer/member, access-loss, partial rollout, rollback, and rerun tests exist
- [ ] Canaries, batches, convergence gates, stop conditions, maintenance window, and acceptance defined
- [ ] Actual device/controller, management, control-plane, data-plane, workload, and residual-risk reporting defined
