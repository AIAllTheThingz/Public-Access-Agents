# Cisco Networking Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Stable device, site, role, owner, PID/hardware, serial/stable ID, and environment recorded
- [ ] IOS, IOS XE, NX-OS, or IOS XR family, release, boot image/packages, and mode verified
- [ ] Controller/Dashboard, site/fabric/template, source of truth, local drift, and conflict policy mapped
- [ ] StackWise, VSS, vPC, chassis, supervisors, route processors, gateways, and redundant paths inventoried
- [ ] Interfaces, optics, VLAN/VRF, STP, EtherChannel, routing, overlay, ACL, QoS/CoPP, and dependencies mapped
- [ ] AAA, certificates, management VRF, console/OOB, logging, telemetry, and break glass defined
- [ ] Hardware/software/ROMMON/controller/API/license lifecycle, support, security, and review date recorded
- [ ] Archive/checkpoint, boot fallback, configuration restore, spare, rollback, and escalation tested or bounded
- [ ] Syntax/model, topology, controller conflict, HA, access-loss, partial rollout, rollback, and rerun tests exist
- [ ] Canaries, batches, convergence gates, stop conditions, maintenance window, and acceptance defined
- [ ] Actual owner/startup/running, management, control/data-plane, workload, and risk reporting defined
