# Terraform and OpenTofu Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Engine, versions, providers, modules, and lock strategy are declared.
- [ ] Backend, state ownership, access, backup, and recovery are documented.
- [ ] Environment, identity, approval, and deployment boundaries are explicit.
- [ ] Format, validate, lint, policy, test, plan, and verification checks are configured.
- [ ] Secret, plan, state, privilege, replacement, and destroy controls are documented.
- [ ] Completion evidence is integrated and maintainer-reviewed.
