# SQL Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Engine, versions, compatibility, collation, and time-zone assumptions are declared.
- [ ] Migration, transaction, locking, and recovery strategies are documented.
- [ ] Security, tenant, role, and sensitive-data boundaries are identified.
- [ ] Lint, isolated migration, test, plan, and integrity checks are configured.
- [ ] Data volume and operational impact are considered.
- [ ] Completion evidence is integrated and maintainer-reviewed.
