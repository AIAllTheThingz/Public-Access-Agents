# Bash Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Interpreter and portability target are explicit.
- [ ] External commands and privileges are documented.
- [ ] Syntax, ShellCheck, and test commands are configured.
- [ ] Dry-run, confirmation, idempotency, cleanup, and rollback behavior are defined.
- [ ] Injection and secret-handling controls are documented.
- [ ] Completion evidence is integrated and maintainer-reviewed.
