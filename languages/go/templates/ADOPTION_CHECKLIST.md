# Go Adoption Checklist

- [ ] Go and toolchain versions are declared.
- [ ] Module, dependency, and generated-code strategies are documented.
- [ ] Concurrency ownership and shutdown rules are adopted.
- [ ] Formatting, vet, test, race, and build commands are configured.
- [ ] Trust boundaries and secret handling are documented.
- [ ] Completion evidence is integrated and maintainer-reviewed.
