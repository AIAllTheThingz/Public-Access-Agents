# Governance Review Checklist

- [ ] Stable IDs are preserved or migration is documented.
- [ ] Authority and accountability are explicit.
- [ ] Precedence is unambiguous.
- [ ] Risk controls are proportionate.
- [ ] Evidence expectations are testable.
- [ ] Decision gates are clear.
- [ ] Exception boundaries do not waive law, safety, authorization, or honesty.
- [ ] Templates do not weaken policy.
- [ ] Examples remain fictitious and non-production.
- [ ] Human approval is not assigned to an agent.
- [ ] Sensitive or vulnerability information is protected.
- [ ] Downstream packages and examples are updated.
- [ ] Compatibility and effective-date impact are recorded.
- [ ] Links, IDs, JSON, and manifests validate.
- [ ] Checks not run and limitations are disclosed.
